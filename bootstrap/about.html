Get to know us!

Simba Trailers was established in 2005 by veteran transporters to serve the growing transport sector in East and Central Africa. The company is located in Dar es Salaam , Tanzania and has its workshop in Tabata Industrial Zone. We currently manufacture flatbed, skeleton, drawbar and lowbed trailers. We use BPW Axles with WABCO Brake Systems and JOST Accessories. We also do extensive repair jobs on old trailers where we do body work and can change the suspension systems. Our trailers are designed and manufactured with Africa in mind. With different specifications for different terrains we can tailor a trailer to your needs.
